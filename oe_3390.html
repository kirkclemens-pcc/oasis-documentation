Edit Text PATIENT AGE
a) Patient age at the time the assessment was completed cannot be more than 140 years.
b) Patient age at the time the assessment was completed cannot be less than 18 years.
c) If M0066_PAT_BIRTH_DT is equal to [--------], then age cannot be computed and this edit does not apply.

Patient age is computed as follows:

1) If both the month and day in M0066_PAT_BIRTH_DT are unknown, replace the month with "07" and the day with "02" for the purpose of calculating age.

2) If only the day in M0066_PAT_BIRTH_DT is unknown, replace the day with the "15" for the purpose of calculating age.

3) Provisionally compute age (in years) by subtracting the year in M0066_PAT_BIRTH_DT from the year in M0090_INFO_COMPLETED_DT.

4) If the month in M0090_INFO_COMPLETED_DT is less than the month in M0066_PAT_BIRTH_DT, then subtract 1 from the provisional age computed in (3) above.

5) If the month in M0090_INFO_COMPLETED_DT is equal to the month in M0066_PAT_BIRTH_DT and if the day in M0090_INFO_COMPLETED_DT is less than the day in M0066_PAT_BIRTH_DT, then subtract 1 from the provisional age computed in (3) above.
