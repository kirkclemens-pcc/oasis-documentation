Edit Text CALCULATION OF EFFECTIVE DATE
a) If M0100_ASSMT_REASON=[01], then ASMT_EFF_DATE is equal to M0030_START_CARE_DT.

b) If M0100_ASSMT_REASON=[03], then ASMT_EFF_DATE is equal to M0032_ROC_DT.

d) If M0100_ASSMT_REASON=[06,07,08,09], then ASMT_EFF_DATE is equal to M0906_DC_TRAN_DTH_DT.
